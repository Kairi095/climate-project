Climate Change & Flooding

Understanding the Impact and How We Can Adapt

Visual Representation

This diorama shows the difference between communities before and after the effects of climate change, especially flooding.

Important Facts

🌧️ Flooding is becoming more frequent and intense due to climate change.
🌍 Rising sea levels and extreme weather patterns increase the risk of floods in coastal and urban areas.
🧑‍🔬 According to the UN, floods affect more people globally than any other natural disaster.

What Can We Do?

  • Promote proper waste disposal to prevent clogged waterways.
  • Support green spaces and sustainable infrastructure.
  • Reduce carbon emissions and energy usage.
⚠️ Flood Risk Alert: If your community lies in a low-lying or coastal area, always stay updated with local flood warnings and evacuation plans.
